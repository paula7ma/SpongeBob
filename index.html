Unlike the life's of his neighbor-Squidward Tentacles who is constanlty being enoyed by SpongeBob and his buddy- Patric.

SpongeBob at his work.

SpongeBob's characteristics

  1. He is always happy.
  2. He is grateful for little things in life.
  3. He pays attention to his inner world and works on himself.
  4. He is loyal and friendly.
  5. He is not ashamed of showing emotions and being vulnerable.
  6. ...Yet he is completely naive and probably suffers from a Peter Pan Syndrome.

Friends

SpongeBob is extremely friendly and lacks the ability to say "no". Therefore he always has somebody to spend time with and quite often finds himself in trouble.
